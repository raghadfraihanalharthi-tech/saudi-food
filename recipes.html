Kabsa

Traditional Saudi rice dish with chicken and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook chicken with spices
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Add rice
  4. Cook until ready

Mandi

Delicious meat and rice dish.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Prepare rice
  3. Serve together

Jareesh

Dish made from crushed wheat.

Ingredients:

  • Crushed wheat
  • Milk
  • Onion

Steps:

  1. Cook wheat
  2. Add milk
  3. Cook until soft
