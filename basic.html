History of Saudi Cuisine

Saudi cuisine has a long history that goes back hundreds of years. It was influenced by desert life and traditional lifestyle. People used simple ingredients like wheat, rice, dates, and meat.

Over time, Saudi food developed because of trade and travel. New spices and cooking methods came from different countries. This made Saudi food rich and full of flavor.
